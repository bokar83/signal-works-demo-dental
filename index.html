Our Story

Dentistry Designed Around Your Child, Not the Other Way Around

We started Bright Smiles because we knew pediatric dentistry could feel less like a chore and more like a highlight of the week. Our office is built from the floor up for kids: from the jellyfish aquarium in the lobby to the ceiling TVs above every chair. Every detail is intentional.

Dr. Maya Chen and her team have served over 2,400 Salt Lake City families, and we're just getting started. We believe the habits formed here last a lifetime.

  • Specially trained in child psychology and behavior management
  • Nitrous oxide sedation for anxious patients
  • Fully digital X-rays with 90% less radiation
  • Online scheduling in under 2 minutes

FAQ

Parents Ask. We Answer.

No dental jargon. No confusing explanations. Just honest answers from people who genuinely love caring for kids.

The American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry recommends a child's first dental visit by their first birthday or within 6 months of their first tooth erupting. Early visits help us establish a dental home and catch any concerns early.

We use the 'tell-show-do' technique, child-friendly language, ceiling TVs, and genuine patience. We never rush a child. For higher anxiety, we offer nitrous oxide (laughing gas) sedation as a safe and gentle option.
