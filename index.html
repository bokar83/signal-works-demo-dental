What We Do

Complete care for every little smile

From baby's first tooth to teenage braces consultations — everything your child needs under one joyful roof.

✨
01

Cleanings & Exams

Gentle hygienists who turn cleanings into a game. We use kid-sized instruments, flavored polish, and lots of high-fives. Every visit ends with a prize from the Treasure Chest.

  • Digital X-rays with 90% less radiation
  • Personalized oral hygiene coaching
  • Fluoride treatment included
🦷
02

Cavity Treatment

Tooth-colored fillings placed so gently, kids often don't realize we did anything. No scary drills, no lectures — just swift, kind care that gets them back to being kids.

  • Tooth-colored composite fillings
  • Topical numbing before any injection
  • Distraction with ceiling TVs
😴
03

Laughing Gas Sedation

Safe, gentle nitrous oxide sedation for anxious patients or complex procedures. Wears off in minutes. No lingering grogginess. Your child walks out the door happy.

  • Nitrous oxide approved by AAP
  • Continuous monitoring throughout
  • Fully reversible in 5 minutes
🛡️
04

Sealants & Fluoride

Invisible armor against cavities. Sealants bond to the chewing surfaces of molars and protect them for years. Fluoride varnish strengthens enamel after every cleaning.

  • Applied in under 5 minutes
  • Lasts 5–10 years
  • Covered by most insurance plans
🚨
05

Emergency Care

Chipped tooth on a Saturday? Toothache at 7pm? We offer same-day emergency appointments. Call us first. We'll pick up — and we'll fit your child in.

  • Same-day slots reserved daily
  • After-hours phone line
  • Tooth reimplantation specialists
😁
06

Orthodontic Evaluation

We identify spacing, bite, and alignment issues early — when correction is easiest and most effective. We partner with top orthodontists in Salt Lake City for seamless referrals.

  • AAO-recommended at age 7
  • Early intervention saves money later
  • Trusted ortho referral network
